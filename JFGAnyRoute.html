Jet Force Gemini / Star Twins Any% Speedrun Route

Version History:

  • 1.0 - Adapted from previous guide and updated to follow current route

Notes:

  • This route uses the Japanese version of the game that utilizes cutscenes skips, saving roughly 10-11 minutes over the run. Some menu navigation and order or levels is based on the ability to skip certain cutscenes. If you are playing on the English version of the game, this may cause some time to be lost.
  • Start timer upon selecting “END” when starting a new file.
  • End timer upon losing control of Floyd in the final Asteroid mission.
  • The labels for individual areas/rooms come from the Nintendo Power Strategy Guide.

Control Tips:

  • Use the “Expert Controls” setup found in the options menu
  • Strafe running is ~15% faster than normal running (same applies to flying)
  • Lupus cannot strafe fly.
  • Pressing start when talking to an NPC exits the conversation.
  • Jetpack controls:
  • C-Up can be used to gain height. This uses more fuel than hovering.
  • C-Down can be used to hover and maintain a specific height.
  • To strafe fly you MUST be strafe running on the ground then jump into the air, once in the air you will no longer need to hold C-Left/C-Right as it will be held for you until you land on the ground again.
  • C-Up/Down also have the same effects when swimming with Vela.

Lupus 1: Boss - Mechantids                            [L1-BOME]

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

        Boss Fight

  • This description assumes you succeed in each cycle of the fight.
  • Start fight by positioning yourself slightly to the right and tilting camera up slightly
  • As soon as it becomes targetable, shoot a Tri-Rocket at Mechantid 1’s right hand and immediately switch to Machine Gun and continue shooting it
  • Mechantid 1 will jump to the left platform and raise his left arm. Shoot it with 2 Tri-Rockets when it becomes targetable.
  • Mechantid 2 will jump to the center platform. Shoot it’s right hand with two Tri-Rockets as soon as it becomes targetable.
  • Mechantid 2 will stay on the center platform and raise his left arm. Shoot it with 2 Tri-Rockets when it becomes targetable.
  • Mechantid 1 will jump to the center platform. Shoot his tail with 2 Tri-Rockets when it becomes targetable. It should be around the same position on his left side as the previous shots you took.
  • Mechantid 2 will jump to the center platform. Shoot his tail with 2 Tri-Rockets when it becomes targetable. It will be on his right side.
  • Both Mechantids will now take turns jumping to the center platform. Shoot each of their heads with 3 Tri-Rocket shots each time they do.

        Thorax F

  • Hover across lava and run to exit and ship

Mizar 1: Boss - Mizar 1                                    [M1-BOMI]

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

        Boss Fight

  • Shoot Mizar in the face when he’s not covering it
  • His laser vision attack is the easiest to 1 cycle, and it can be worth it to wait for him to do this attack (1/3 chance each attack) because of how long it takes for him to uncover his face.
